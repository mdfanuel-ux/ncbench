Flue-cured tobacco production in North Carolina — part of the NCBench project

Overview

North Carolina is one of the top flue-cured tobacco producing states in the US. Tobacco has been a cornerstone of NC agriculture for over 400 years and remains economically significant — particularly in the eastern Coastal Plain counties. Organic tobacco production in NC accounts for approximately 2,680 acres on 61 farms, with a farm gate value exceeding $19 million.
