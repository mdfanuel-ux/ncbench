Corn production in North Carolina — part of the NCBench project

Overview

North Carolina plants 910,000 acres of corn annually, making it the #2 field crop after soybean. Corn is grown for grain (144 bu/acre avg yield) and silage. It's a key rotation partner with soybean — most NC farmers grow both. The CROP dataset already contains 25,259 corn QA pairs from Chinese agriculture; NCBench adds NC-specific organic corn production knowledge.
