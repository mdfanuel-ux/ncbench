How AgriRegion's spatial-semantic scoring works across NC counties

Haversine Distance & Spatial Scoring

AgriRegion uses a distance decay function to prioritize local Extension documents. Documents closer to the user get higher spatial scores.

Sdistance = 1 / (1 + d(guser, gdoc) / Dmax)

Sfinal = (1 − α) · Ssemantic + α · Sdistance    where α = 0.5, Dmax = 500 km

What This Means for Retrieval

α = 0.5 (default)
Equal weight to semantic relevance and spatial proximity. A document from the user's county gets a 50% boost from location alone.
α = 0 (semantic only)
No spatial component. All documents ranked purely by content similarity. This is the ablation baseline.
α = 1 (spatial only)
Pure proximity ranking. Closest documents always win regardless of content. Not useful alone but shows spatial signal.
