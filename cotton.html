Overview

North Carolina is a significant cotton-producing state, with production concentrated in the Coastal Plain counties. Cotton is often grown in rotation with peanuts, soybeans, and corn. The NC Extension cotton portal provides timely guidance on variety selection, pest management, defoliation, and harvest aids.
