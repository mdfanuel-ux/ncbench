Overview

North Carolina is a top-5 strawberry producing state, with production concentrated in the Piedmont and Sandhills regions. Most NC strawberries are grown using plasticulture (raised beds with plastic mulch) for fresh market sales. Key challenges include anthracnose, Botrytis gray mold, spotted wing drosophila, and spider mites. The Extension strawberry portal provides seasonal guidance on variety selection, pest management, and tunnel production.
